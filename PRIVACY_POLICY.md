# Privacy Policy for Cloud Clipboard

**Last Updated:** 12/31/2025

## What Data We Collect

Cloud Clipboard collects and stores the following information:

- **Account Information**: Username and password (passwords are securely hashed and salted)
- **Clipboard Content**: Text and images you upload through the extension or web app along with the date of upload.

## How We Use Your Data

- Your account credentials are used to authenticate you and secure your clipboard data
- Your most recent upload is stored to enable syncing between your devices
- We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to third parties

## Data Storage

- Only your most recent upload is stored at any time
- Each new upload replaces the previous one
- Passwords are securely hashed, salted and never stored in plain text
- Data is stored on our servers at Google Cloud

## Data Retention

- Your clipboard content is retained until you upload new content (which replaces it)
- Account data is retained until you request deletion
- You can delete your account at any time by sending me an email jhiggins2@stevens.edu 

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Request information about how your data is used

## Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including password hashing and secure data transmission. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Third-Party Services

Cloud Clipboard does not integrate with or share data with third-party services.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
